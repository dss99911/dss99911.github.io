# Hindi Nouns (힌디어 명사)

힌디어 명사에 대한 종합 가이드입니다.

## Gender in Nouns (명사의 성)

### Masculine Nouns (남성 명사)

**Regular Pattern: -aa ending**

#### Irregular Masculine Nouns

| Hindi | Romanization | Meaning | Note |
|-------|-------------|---------|------|
| पलंग | palang | bed | 이- 발음 없음 |
| अनानास | anaanaas | pineapple | aa 발음 있음 |
| सेब | seb | apple | |
| घर | ghar | house | |

### Feminine Nouns (여성 명사)

**Regular Pattern: -ee ending**

#### Irregular Feminine Nouns

여성형 단어는 이- 발음과 같이 발음하기 쉬운 단어가 많음

| Hindi | Romanization | Meaning | Note |
|-------|-------------|---------|------|
| मेज़ | mez | table | |
| किताब | kitaab | book | 이- 발음 있음 |
| माता | maataa | mom | 엄마 |
| भेड़ | bher | sheep | 순해서 |

## Plural Forms (복수형)

### Summary

| Type | Rule | Example |
|------|------|---------|
| Masculine (aa ending) | aa -> e | कुत्ता (kuttaa) -> कुत्ते (kutte) - dog |
| Masculine (not aa) | no change | अख़बार (akhbaar) -> अख़बार (akhbaar) - newspaper |
| Feminine (ee ending) | ee -> iyan | मछली (machlee) -> मछलियाँ (machliyaan) - fish |
| Feminine (not ee) | + en | मेज़ (mez) -> मेज़ें (mezen) - table |

## Pronouns (대명사)

### First Person (1인칭)

| Hindi | Romanization | Meaning |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| मैं | main | I |
| हम | ham | We |

### Second Person (2인칭)

| Hindi | Romanization | Meaning | Usage |
|-------|-------------|---------|-------|
| तू | too | You | Intimate - 엄청 친밀한 경우나 어린아이에게 |
| तुम | tum | You | Informal |
| आप | aap | You | Formal |

### Third Person (3인칭)

| Hindi | Romanization | Meaning |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| यह | yah | He/She/It/This |
| वह | vah | He/She/It/That |
| ये | ye | These/They |
| वे | ve | Those/They |

## Possessive Pronouns (소유 대명사)

### का (kaa) - ~의

뒤에 오는 명사에 따라 바뀐다:

| Gender | Form |
|--------|------|
| 남성 | kaa |
| 여성 (단수/복수) | kee |
| 남성 복수 | ke |

#### Examples

**बिल्ली का खाना** (billee kaa khaanaa)
- The cat's food

**नेहा की बेटी** (nehaa kee betee)
- Neha's daughter

---

### अपना (apnaa) - ~자신의

자기 자신의 것을 표현할 때 사용

**मैं अपना पानी पीती हूँ** (main apnaa paanee peetee hoon)
- I drink my water (said by a female)

---

## Oblique Case (사격/간접격)

간접격, 후치사와 결합하여, 부사를 만듬

후치사와 결합할 경우, 아래와 같이 변형됨.

### Summary

- **단수 변경 x** (남성 단수 aa만 변경: aa -> e)
- **복수에는 on이 붙음**

---

### Masculine Type 1 (aa ending)

| Form | Rule | Example |
|------|------|---------|
| Singular | aa -> e | लड़का (larkaa) -> लड़के (larke) - boy |
| Singular | aa -> e | केला (kelaa) -> केले (kele) - banana |
| Plural | e -> on | लड़के (larke) -> लड़कों (larkon) - boys |
| Plural | e -> on | केले (kele) -> केलों (kelon) - bananas |

### Masculine Type 2 (not aa ending)

| Form | Rule | Example |
|------|------|---------|
| Singular | no change | पलंग (palang) - bed |
| Plural | + on | पलंग (palang) -> पलंगों (palangon) - beds |

### Feminine Type 1 (ee ending)

| Form | Rule | Example |
|------|------|---------|
| Singular | no change | चाबी (caabee) - key |
| Plural | iyaan -> iyon | चाबियाँ (caabiyaan) -> चाबियों (caabiyon) - keys |

### Feminine Type 2 (not ee ending)

| Form | Rule | Example |
|------|------|---------|
| Singular | no change | किताब (kitaab) - book |
| Plural | en -> on | किताबें (kitaaben) -> किताबों (kitaabon) - books |

## Special Pronouns

### कुछ (kuch) - Something, Some (plural)

**कुछ पलंग पर है** (kuch palang par hai)
- Something is on the bed

**कुछ लड़के केले खाते हैं** (kuch larke kele khaate hain)
- **Some** boys eat bananas.

---

### कोई (koee) - Someone, Some (singular)

**कोई पलंग पर है** (koee palang par hai)
- **Someone** is on the bed

**कोई लड़का अच्छा खाना खा रहा है** (koee larkaa acchaa khaanaa khaa rahaa hai)
- **Some** boy is eating good food.

### Oblique Case of कोई

**कोई (koee) -> किसी (kisee)**

**किसी ने शब्दकोश ख़रीदा** (kisee ne shabdkosh khareedaa)
- Someone bought a dictionary

## ही (hee) - Only

Reference: [Learning Hindi - ही hee Part 1](http://www.learning-hindi.com/post/25803859206/lesson-131-%E0%A4%B9-hee-only), [Part 2](http://www.learning-hindi.com/post/26588748266/lesson-132-%E0%A4%B9-hee-only-part-2)

**only** 의 의미

in, ham 등의 받침이 있는 경우 **een**으로 끝나고, 그외에는 **ee**로 끝남

| Base | + ही | Result | Meaning |
|------|------|--------|---------|
| हम (ham) | ही (hee) | हमीं (hameen) | Just us |
| इन (in) | ही (hee) | इन्हीं (inheen) | Just them |
| उस (us) | ही (hee) | उसी (usee) | Just him/her/it |
