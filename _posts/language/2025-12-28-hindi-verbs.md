# Hindi Verbs (힌디어 동사)

힌디어 동사에 대한 종합 가이드입니다.

## Verb Basics (동사 기초)

### Aspect Markers (완료 여부)

| Marker | Meaning | Korean |
|--------|---------|--------|
| 따 (taa) | Incomplete/Habitual | 미완료 |
| 아 (aa) | Completed | 완료 |
| 라하 (rahaa) | Ongoing | 진행 |

### Tense Markers (시점)

| Marker | Meaning | Korean |
|--------|---------|--------|
| 해 (hai) | Present | 현재 |
| 타 (thaa) | Past | 과거 |

## Be Verb (be 동사 - होना honaa)

### Key Points

- 인칭, 수에 따라 변화됨. 성별과는 상관 없음
- 1인칭 단수, 1인칭 복수
- 2인칭 단수, 2인칭 복수/존칭
- 3인칭

### Present Tense Conjugation

| Hindi | Romanization | English | Note |
|-------|-------------|---------|------|
| मैं हूँ | main hoon | I am | |
| हम हैं | ham hain | We are | |
| तू है | too hai | You are (Intimate) | 엄청 친밀한 경우, 상대를 그대라고 칭하는 것처럼 |
| आप हैं | aap hain | You are (Formal) | 여럿이 있을 때 존칭을 사용하듯, 상대에게 존칭 |
| तुम हो | tum ho | You are (Casual) | |

### Third Person

| Hindi | Romanization | English |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| यह है | yeh hai | He/She/This is (Near) |
| ये हैं | ye hain | These/They are (Near) |
| वह है | voh hai | He/She/That is (Far) |
| वे हैं | ve hain | Those/They are (Far) |

### Summary

- **Hai** - 3인칭 (is)
- **Hain** - 존칭 (are)
- **Hoon** - 자신 (am)
- **Ho** - 상대

---

### Past Tense (과거형)

| Hindi | Romanization | English | Usage |
|-------|-------------|---------|-------|
| था | thaa | was | Singular Masculine |
| थे | the | were | Plural Masculine |
| थी | thee | was | Singular Feminine |
| थीं | theen | were | Plural Feminine |

#### Examples

**मैं घर पर था** (main ghar par thaa)
- I was at home (said by a male)

**आप कहाँ थे?** (aap kahaan the?)
- Where were you?

**ओबामा भारत में थे** (obaamaa bhaarat men the)
- Obama was in India. (존칭으로써서 the)

## Regular Verbs (일반 동사)

### Key Rules

주어(subject)의 성별/수에 따라 형태가 달라짐:
- 남성
- 남성 복수
- 여성

### Root Form (원형)

**-ना (naa)**
- सीखना (seekhnaa) - to learn

---

### Present Tense (현재형)

| Ending | Usage | Example |
|--------|-------|---------|
| ~ता (taa) | Masculine Singular | मैं खाता हूँ (main khaataa hoon) - I eat (male) |
| ~ते (te) | Masculine Plural | हम पीते हैं (ham peete hain) - We drink |
| ~ती (tee) | Feminine (Singular/Plural) | वह पढ़ती है (voh parhtee hai) - She reads |

---

### Present Continuous Tense (현재 진행형)

| Ending | Usage | Example |
|--------|-------|---------|
| रहा (rahaa) | Masculine Singular | मैं पाकिस्तान जा रहा हूँ (main paakistaan jaa rahaa hoon) - I am going to Pakistan (male) |
| रहे (rahe) | Masculine Plural | हम लिख रहे हैं (ham likh rahe hain) - We are writing |
| रही (rahee) | Feminine | तुम हिन्दी नहीं बोल रही हो (tum hindee naheen bol rahee ho) - You are not speaking Hindi (female) |

---

### Past Continuous Tense (과거 진행형)

hai를 thaa로 변경. thaa는 be동사의 과거형 참조

**मैं चल रहा हूँ** (main cal rahaa hoon)
- I am walking (male)

**यह समोसे खा रही थी** (yeh samose khaa rahee thee)
- She was eating samosas.

**अनिल सो रहा था** (anil so rahaa thaa)
- Anil was sleeping.

> Note: rahaa를 다 발음안하고 ra만 발음 하는 경우도 있음
> - main cal ra thaa

---

### Future Tense (미래형)

**-naa hai -> (e: hai에 따라 달라짐(인칭)) g (aa: naa의 변형에 따라 달라짐(성별))**

일반동사는 hai와 같이 쓰는데, 이건 hai와 같이 쓰지 않는 대신에, hai와 비슷하게 성별에 따라서도 형태 변환이 일어난다.

#### First Person

| Type | Formation | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Masculine Singular | naa + oon = oongaa | मैं -ऊँगा (main -oongaa) - I will |
| Feminine | nee + oon = oongee | मैं -ऊँगी (main -oongee) - I will |
| Masculine Plural | ne + hain = enge | हम -एँगे (ham -enge) - We will |
| Feminine Plural | nee + hain = engee | आप -एँगी (aap -engee) - You will (Formal) |

#### Second Person

| Type | Formation | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Singular (too hai) | naa + hai = egaa | तू -एगा (too -egaa) - You will (Very Informal) |
| Singular (tum ho) | Exception: naa + ho = oge | तुम -ओगे (tum -oge) - You will (Informal) |
| Plural (aap hain) | ne + hain = enge | आप -एँगे (aap -enge) - You will (Formal) |

> Note: tum -oge is an exception to the normal rules

#### Third Person

| Type | Formation | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Singular | naa + hai = egaa | यह/वह -एगा (yeh/voh -egaa) - He/It will |
| Plural | ne + hain = enge | ये/वे -एँगे (ye/ve -enge) - They will |

---

### Imperative Verb (명령형)

Add **इए (ie)** to the root

**सेब को खाइए** (seb ko khaaie)
- Please eat the apple.

#### Irregular Forms

| Root | Meaning | Imperative |
|------|---------|------------|
| करना (karnaa) | to do | कीजिए (keejie) - Please do |
| लेना (lenaa) | to take | लीजिए (leejie) - Please take |
| देना (denaa) | to give | दीजिए (deejie) - Please give |
| पीना (peenaa) | to drink | पीजिए (peejie) - Please drink |

## Auxiliary Verbs (조동사)

### Can (सकना saknaa)

**सीमा हिन्दी बोल सकती है** (seemaa hindee bol saktee hai)
- Seema can speak Hindi

### Can Not

**नहीं, तुम वह नहीं खा सकती** (naheen, tum voh naheen khaa saktee)
- No, you can't eat that.

## Tense Summary Table (시제 정리표)

| Tense | Singular | Plural/Honorific |
|-------|----------|------------------|
| Present Continuous | main kar raha hu | aap kar rahe ho |
| Present (Habitual) | main kar ta hu | |
| Past (dia: give) | maine kar dia hai | kya apne kar dia hai? |
| Past (lia: take/receive) | maine kha lia hai | kya apne kha lia hai? |
| Past (ya: motion) | jana -> gaya, chala gaya | chale gaye |
| Future (unga) | main karunga, main hindi padhunga | aap kam karenge |

### Notes on Past Tense

- **타, 리아, 디아, 가야** - 과거형
- **타는 be동사 과거형, 따는 do동사 현재형**
- **Bolna -> bola** (불규칙 과거형)

### I'm going to do

**Main karne hi wala tha** (Present continuous intention)

### Let's do something

- **milte hai** - Let's meet
- **chalie** - Let's go
